We have a commitment to the following principles:

  • No advertisements, we provide a service and get paid for it.
    Users here are not products.
  • Our platform is made with a moderation first philosophy: the tools for both automatic and user generated moderation have top priority in our development. See growth & moderation.
  • Machines are citizens too; we make it clear which accounts are computer programs and welcome them. AI generated content will be marked as such. See our machine policy.
  • We aim to be a tool for improving mental health online, being a platform that's slower than others, and doesn't predate on attention.
  • We give users total control of their feeds, no algorithm shenanigans.
  • We don't mine user's data to keep or sell to anybody.
    Our privacy policies are as simple as possible, as user protective as possible.
  • As a paid platform we understand that this may limit the availability of participation from people who can't afford it, so we will always have a sponsorship program, where each paying customer will be effectively paying for another, sponsored user. See our sponsorship program.
